About the Camp

    The 2020 Mid-Atlantic Camp Session Includes:
  • Four nights lodging in air-condition dormitories
  • 12 meals in the Franklin Dining Hall
  • Accident Insurance for every Student, Chaperone, and Band Director
  • A marked drill field for each band's sole use
  • A minimum of twelve (12) practice sessions
  • An assigned indoor rehearsal space
  • Free room and board for band director and their family
  • Free room and board for up to eight (8) chaperones, depending on size of band
  • Planned, supervised recreational activities
  • A Saturday morning band exhibition on the Astro-Turfed field at Adams Stadium (Family and friends are invited to attend the exhibition.)

About Ferrum College

Our 2020 Marching Band Camp will be held at Ferrum College. Founded in 1913, Ferrum is a well-known four-year, private, co-educational, liberal arts college related to the United Methodist Church. Ferrum College offers many varied and nationally recognized bachelor's programs. Its beautiful 770 acre campus is located in the rural foothills of the Virginia mountains near the Blue Ridge Parkway, Smith Mountain Lake, Fairy Stone State Park, and the VA cities of Rocky Mount, and Roanoke (35 miles NE) and the NC city of Greensboro (70 miles South).
